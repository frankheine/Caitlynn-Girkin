# Caitlynn-Girkin Case Dossier

An interactive web-based case study interface for **State of Illinois v. Caitlynn J. Girkin** (Case No. 26-CF-1099), a first-degree murder litigation case filed in Tazewell County Circuit Court.

## Overview

This dossier presents a comprehensive, interactive examination of the criminal investigation, forensic evidence, and judicial proceedings related to the March 10, 2026 incident in which Caitlynn J. Girkin, a former Creve Coeur police officer, fatally shot her roommate, Adolfo Cazares.

## Case Information

- **Case Number:** 26-CF-1099
- **Court:** Tazewell County Circuit Court, Pekin, Illinois
- **Presiding Judge:** Chris Doscotch
- **Defendant:** Caitlynn J. Girkin (former police officer, Creve Coeur PD)
- **Victim:** Adolfo Cazares, age 29
- **Charge:** First-Degree Murder
- **Arrest Status:** Remanded to Pretrial Detention Without Bond
- **Arraignment Date:** August 6, 2026

## Features

### Interactive Navigation
- **Shifting Stories:** Analyze four distinct narrative versions provided by the defendant throughout the investigation, with prosecution refutations and credibility assessments
- **Forensic Timeline:** Chronological reconstruction of March 10, 2026, with audio-guided evidence playback
- **Badge & Vehicle Record:** Documentation of defendant's police misconduct history, including DUI arrest and unauthorized vehicle operation
- **Chem Tox Profile:** Interactive toxicology simulator showing the combined effects of cocaine, ketamine, and alcohol on defendant behavior
- **Ruling & Verdict Deck:** Judicial detention order and Judge Doscotch's final determination

### Advanced Interface
- **Audio Briefing System:** Native Web Speech API integration for narrative vocalization
- **WebGL Background:** Dynamic 3D volumetric tunnel shader rendering
- **Real-Time Visualizer:** Equalizer bar animation synchronized with audio playback
- **Dark Theme UI:** Premium glassmorphism design with optimized contrast and readability
